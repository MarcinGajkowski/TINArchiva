What is Toyotathon?

So, what is Toyotathon? It is a yearly event that showcases extremely low leasing and financing rates on some of your favorite Toyota automobiles. Folks come from Palm Beach Gardens and beyond to take advantage of these offers.
The specific offers that will be featured during Toyotathon haven’t been announced yet, but keep watching our website for details, along with Toyotathon 2020-2021 dates.

When is Toyotathon? Toyotathon normally takes place from mid-November through early January. The exact dates for Toyotathon 2020-2021 haven’t been announced yet.
But you can bet we will let you know as soon as they are. Keep watching this site for Toyotathon dates.
